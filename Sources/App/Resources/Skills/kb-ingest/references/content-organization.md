# Content Organization Guidelines

Unlike the type-based subdirectories mentioned in some steps, the actual vault organizes content by topic. Choose the subdirectory that best matches the content's topic:

- **AI/ML:** AI research, machine learning, large language models, neural networks
- **Tech:** Programming, software development, cybersecurity, hardware, general tech news
- **Business:** Market analysis, financial news, stock/crypto prices, investment research, mergers & acquisitions
- **Swift:** iOS/Swift development, Apple ecosystem, mobile apps
- **NBA/WWE/TV and Movies:** Entertainment and sports content
- **Health:** Medical research, fitness, nutrition, wellness
- **Careers:** Job search, professional development, career advice
- **Books:** Book reviews, summaries, reading recommendations

For mixed or general content, use `raw/AI` or `raw/Tech` as defaults. When in doubt, prioritize the most specific matching subdirectory.
