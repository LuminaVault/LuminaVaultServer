# Product Strategy

This umbrella skill consolidates two strategic analysis capabilities:

- **Brand legitimacy investigation** — systematic web-based verification of product/brand credibility
- **Freemium tier mapping** — aligning feature sets with Free/Pro/Premium tiers and keeping documentation in sync

## When to use

Apply when evaluating new products, market research, or refining the monetization structure of your own product.
