Common topics

  • Account & sign-in — trouble with email, phone, Apple, or Google sign-in.
  • Subscriptions — billing, restoring purchases, or cancelling. Manage or cancel anytime in your Apple ID → Subscriptions. Refunds are handled by Apple.
  • Data & privacy — export or delete your data in the app (Settings). See our Privacy Policy.
  • Health, Photos & permissions — manage access in iOS Settings → Privacy.
  • Bugs & feedback — include your device model, iOS version, and what you were doing.

Cancelling a subscription

Open the Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → LuminaVault → Cancel. Access continues until the end of the current billing period.
